About Me

Personal Background

I'm a first-year college student passionate about technology, creativity, and making meaningful connections. Growing up in Cebu, I've always been fascinated by how things work and love exploring new ideas.

My Interests

When I'm not buried in my books, you'll find me coding side projects, binge-watching anime, or searching online for new books to add to cart. I'm a huge fan of K-pop, love photography, and I'm constantly learning something new.

Personality Highlights

My friends describe me as an enthusiastic and curious person who loves learning and discovering new things. I enjoy meaningful conversations, but I also value quiet time to think and recharge. I'm always ready for an adventure. As an ambivert, books are my comfort zone and they help me explore ideas beyond my own world. I believe in working hard, staying humble, and finding joy in small moments.

Family & Roots

My family is my foundation. We're a close-knit bunch of five - my hardworking parents, two siblings, and me. Sunday family dinners are sacred in our household, and my mom's adobo recipe is unbeatable.

Traditions That Shaped Me

  • Noche Buena: Our Christmas Eve celebration taught me the value of togetherness
  • Sunday Mass: Family time combined with reflection and gratitude
  • Beach Trips: Annual family getaways that created my best childhood memories

I carry forward values of respect, resilience, and bayanihan (community spirit) that my parents instilled in me.
